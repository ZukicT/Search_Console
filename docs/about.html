The story behind Search Console for iOS

Hi, I'm the developer

Solo indie developer

I built this app because I needed it myself. No big company, no VC funding - just one developer solving a problem I face every day.

Why I Built This

I use Search Console to keep tabs on my website, but honestly? The mobile experience is rough. I got tired of jumping into Safari every time I wanted to check something quick.

I saw Google has an API for it, and based on all the forum posts I've read, it doesn't seem like they're planning to build an app anytime soon. The options on the App Store didn't really do it for me either. One looked sketchy, the others just felt off.

"There has to be a better way to check my search data on the go."

So I built my own.

It does what I need. I can check my keywords, see which pages are getting traffic, and keep an eye on Core Web Vitals. Nothing fancy, just the stuff I actually want to see when I open the app.

I'm not a big company. It's just me, building something I use every day. If you've felt the same frustration, maybe it'll work for you too.

What drives this app

Privacy first

Your data stays in your Google account. The app displays what Google collects - I don't store or sell anything. Manage your Google privacy.

Fast and focused

No bloat. Open the app, see your data. Built for quick checks on the go.

Always improving

Your subscription supports active development. New features ship regularly.
