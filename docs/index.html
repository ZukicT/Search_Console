Why I made this app

Google does not offer a mobile app for Search Console. When I looked for one, the options I found either looked sketchy or lacked the features I needed. So I built this app: a native iOS app that connects to your Search Console data, shows top keywords and pages, Core Web Vitals, PDF reports, and push summaries, without the clutter or privacy concerns.

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google LLC.

Subscription

The app requires a subscription to use. It is not optional. Your subscription helps pay for Google API costs (Search Console and PageSpeed Insights) and ongoing app maintenance and development.

7-day free trial, then $5.99/month. You can try the app before you subscribe. Manage or cancel in iPhone Settings under Subscriptions, or at App Store Subscriptions.

Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?
Use the same Google account you use for Google Search Console. No extra setup. If you don't have any Search Console properties yet, add a site at search.google.com/search-console first.
What data does the app use?
The app only shows data that Google Search Console already has for your account. It uses Google's official API. Your data stays in your Google account; the app just displays it.
What about the subscription?
The app requires a subscription. 7-day free trial, then $5.99/month. It helps pay for API costs (Search Console, PageSpeed) and app development and maintenance. Manage or cancel in iPhone Settings under Subscriptions, or at App Store Subscriptions.
I don't see my site.
Make sure the site is added and verified in Google Search Console (web). Only properties you can access there will appear in the app. Try signing out and back in if something is missing.
Core Web Vitals or reports not loading?
Check your internet connection. Some features depend on external APIs. If it keeps failing, pull to refresh or try again later.
