Search Console

Google Search performance on your iPhone. Support and help.

About the app

Search Console for iOS connects to your Google Search Console account so you can view performance, top keywords, top pages, and Core Web Vitals from your phone. Sign in with Google, pick your site, and see how you show up in search.

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google LLC.

Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?
Use the same Google account you use for Google Search Console. No extra setup. If you don't have any Search Console properties yet, add a site at search.google.com/search-console first.
What data does the app use?
The app only shows data that Google Search Console already has for your account. It uses Google's official API. Your data stays in your Google account; the app just displays it.
What about the subscription?
Premium is an optional monthly subscription with a 7-day free trial. You can manage or cancel it in your iPhone Settings under Subscriptions, or at App Store Subscriptions.
I don't see my site.
Make sure the site is added and verified in Google Search Console (web). Only properties you can access there will appear in the app. Try signing out and back in if something is missing.
Core Web Vitals or reports not loading?
Check your internet connection. Some features depend on external APIs. If it keeps failing, pull to refresh or try again later.
