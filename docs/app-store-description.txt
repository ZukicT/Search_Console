Google discontinued their official Search Console mobile app, leaving website owners without a native way to check search performance on the go. Search Console for iPhone fills that gap. It's the SEO app for iPhone and Search Console mobile companion you need: monitor your site's Google Search performance from your phone with access to clicks, impressions, rankings, positions, and Core Web Vitals. Whether you manage client sites, run an e-commerce store, or grow a blog, you get the same Google Search Console data and analytics you're used to, in a dedicated iOS app. Use it as your website performance tracker, SEO dashboard for iPhone, and site performance app—all in one.

KEY FEATURES
• All properties in one place – View all your Search Console sites and domains in a single list.
• Search query tracking – See which keywords drive traffic; sort by clicks, CTR, or position. Get the SEO metrics on iPhone that matter.
• Performance analytics – Charts and metrics for 24 hours, 7, 28, or 90 days, and up to 16 months. Stats, statistics, and trends at a glance.
• Core Web Vitals – Track LCP, CLS, and INP, the metrics that affect how Google evaluates your pages.
• PDF reports – Export reports with keyword data, page performance, and device breakdowns.
• Sitemap – List, submit, and manage sitemaps per property.

PRIVACY & SECURITY
Your data stays between you and Google. The app uses a direct OAuth connection to Search Console; no third-party servers and no storage of your Search Console data by us.
